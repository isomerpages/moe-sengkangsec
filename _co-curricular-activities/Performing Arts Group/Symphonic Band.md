<p><strong>CCA Beliefs</strong>
<br>The Symphonic Band (SKY Band) nurtures musical excellence through dedication
and passion. We strive to develop accomplished musicians who not only master
their craft but also embody the values of artistry, leadership, and character.
Through our collective pursuit of musical distinction, members cultivate
resilience, responsibility, forge lasting friendships, and develop the
discipline to excel both in music and in life.</p>
<p><strong>Key Programmes</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Annual band camp</p>

<li>
<p>Master classes with distinguished musicians and music educators</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exchange with other bands</p>
</li>
</ul>
