<p><strong>CCA Beliefs</strong> 
<br>At Seng Kang Modern Dance – Amplify, we are committed to nurturing our
students’ passion for dance while developing a strong sense of identity
and belonging within the CCA.</p>
<p>Through our weekly training sessions, students hone their dance techniques,
explore a variety of genres, and express their creativity through choreography.
These experiences provide a platform for students to grow as confident
individuals who are able to express themselves and communicate effectively
through the art of dance.</p>
<p>We also believe in instilling values such as resilience and perseverance,
as students learn to overcome challenges and strive for excellence both
on and off the stage.</p>
