<p><strong>CCA Beliefs</strong>
<br>Our Media Services Club provides students with the opportunity to be equipped
with skills that are relevant in post-secondary media-related courses.</p>
<p>These skills include operating basic audio systems, photography, videography,
post-production and project management.</p>
<p>Our Media Services Club is vital in supporting every school event, and
ensuring that every programme is carried out well.</p>
