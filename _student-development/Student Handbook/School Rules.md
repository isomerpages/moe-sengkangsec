<h4>POSSIBLE CONSEQUENCES</h4>
<p>Possible consequences for breaching the rules stated above include:</p>
<p>apology, writing a reflection piece, after-school detention, demerit points,
corrective work (service to school), loss of privileges, confiscation/safekeeping
of prohibited items, in-school suspension, out-of-school suspension, expulsion</p>
